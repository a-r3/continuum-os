# Planning Rules

Purpose: enforce plan-first execution and reviewable task records.

Scope: unconditional.

Rules:

- Follow the lifecycle in `governance/CHANGE_PROTOCOL.md`.
- Every execution task needs objective, scope, non-scope, assumptions, risks, sequence, acceptance criteria, validation, evidence, rollback, approval gates, stop conditions, and closeout.
- Plans produce proposals until the Product Owner approves them.
- Do not expand scope silently.
- Before changing files, confirm the active task authorizes the change.
- Preserve stop conditions and rollback notes in task records.
