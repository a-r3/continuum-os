## Owner

Claude Code, after Product Owner transition approval and task activation.

## Objective

Conduct founder onboarding and prepare a ratified product foundation for Continuum OS without relying on Codex chat history or hidden memory.

## Required First Step

Claude Code must perform a read-only comprehension and consistency review before asking founder discovery questions or modifying files.

The first response must cover:

- current project state;
- current phase;
- task state;
- ratified decisions;
- unresolved questions;
- risks;
- explicit non-scope;
- contradictions;
- missing information;
- readiness to begin P01.

## Scope After Activation

- Conduct founder onboarding in controlled question groups.
- Distinguish facts, assumptions, proposals, and ratified decisions.
- Capture proposed decisions without ratifying them.
- Wait for Product Owner ratification before persisting semantic conclusions as accepted facts.
- Prepare the product foundation only after discovery.
- Maintain project state, session history, risks, questions, decisions, and handoff.

## Non-Scope

- Architecture selection outside approved P03 tasks.
- Implementation stack selection.
- Implementation code.
- Autonomous phase transition.
- Commit or push without approval.
- License invention without Product Owner decision.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Product Owner confirms the comprehension review.
- Founder discovery is completed in reviewable groups.
- Proposed decisions are separated from ratified decisions.
- Product foundation is prepared only from confirmed answers and ratified decisions.
- Governance and handoff remain current throughout the task.

## Activation Requirement

This task may become active only after Product Owner approval of the P00 to P01 transition.
